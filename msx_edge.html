Families
Signals are computed on the close and filled at the next session's open, long-only — matching how MSX orders actually execute. The three fixed-TP/SL families (RSI dip, Down-streak, Breakout TP/SL) exit via working intrabar orders: when one bar touches both levels the stop is assumed to fill first, and gaps through a level fill at the open — conservative on purpose. Every candidate is optimised on the first portion of history (in-sample) and judged on the unseen remainder (out-of-sample). Champions are ranked by out-of-sample net return among configs that clear the trade minimum with profit factor ≥ 1.
